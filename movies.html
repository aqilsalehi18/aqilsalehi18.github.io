Horror Movies

Horror is my favorite movie genre. I like horror movies because they are exciting, suspenseful, and sometimes unpredictable.

A good horror movie keeps me interested because I want to know what is going to happen next. I especially enjoy movies with mystery, dark settings, and surprising endings.

What I Like in Horror Movies

  1. Suspenseful stories
  2. Unexpected endings
  3. Scary and dark settings
  4. Interesting characters
